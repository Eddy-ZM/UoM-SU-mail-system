Dear students,

We would like to invite all Department of Chemistry students to our next Student Feedback Forum. The session is an opportunity to share your experience, discuss common concerns and suggest practical improvements.

What you need to know
01Share your experience
Tell us what is working well and where students would benefit from change.
02Hear current updates

03Help set priorities
Your contributions will help guide the topics we take forward in departmental discussions.
